Our German Language Courses: A1 to B1

Our curriculum is based on the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR) to ensure a structured and effective learning path from beginner to intermediate proficiency.

Level A1 (Beginner)

The perfect start for absolute beginners. Learn basic vocabulary, simple phrases, and grammar essentials for everyday situations.

Level A2 (Advanced Beginner)

Build on your foundation to handle more complex daily tasks, communicate about past events, and describe your surroundings.

Level B1 (Intermediate)

Achieve independent language use. You will be able to handle most situations while traveling and express opinions on a variety of topics.
